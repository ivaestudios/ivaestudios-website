FAQ

Questions about hotel TikTok.

Do you run TikTok for hotels specifically?
Yes. The studio specializes in TikTok for hospitality. We shoot vertical video on property, produce trend-aware content and run the channel day-to-day for boutique hotels and resorts.
Is TikTok worth it for luxury hotels?
Yes when produced editorially. TikTok now drives a significant share of travel discovery for under-40 guests. Luxury brands win on TikTok with restraint, atmosphere and storytelling, not on trends alone.
How often do you post on TikTok?
Three to seven posts per week, depending on the plan. TikTok rewards cadence. We balance scheduled hero pieces with trend-responsive videos that capture the moment.
Do you coordinate with creators and influencers?
Yes. We source, brief and manage trusted creators in Cancun, Riviera Maya, Tulum and Los Cabos for hosted stays and content collaborations.
Can you cross-post TikTok to Instagram Reels?
Yes, when it fits the brand. We tailor each cut for platform context. TikTok-native edits stay on TikTok, polished cuts run on Reels.
Do you handle TikTok ads?
Yes. TikTok Ads campaigns are included in the Enterprise plan, with creative production, audience targeting and reporting tied to direct booking goals.
