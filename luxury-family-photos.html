FAQ — Cancún family photographer questions.

What is the best time of day for a family photo session in Cancun?+

Golden hour — the final 60 to 90 minutes before sunset — produces the most flattering, warm light. In Cancun, sunset ranges from approximately 5:45 PM in winter to 7:30 PM in summer. We schedule every session to coincide with this window.

What should my family wear for a resort photo session?+

We send a personalized wardrobe guide after booking. Coordinate around two or three complementary tones — ivory, sand, sage, dusty blue. Avoid neon colors, large logos, and busy patterns. Linen and flowing fabrics work best in ocean breezes.

How do you handle young children and toddlers?+

We use a play-based approach — kids explore and interact naturally while we capture genuine expressions. Sessions are planned around energy levels and nap schedules, with breaks for snacks. We never force poses.

Can you photograph a large multi-generation family reunion?+

Absolutely. We create a shot list that includes the full group, individual family units, grandparents with grandchildren, and sibling groups. For groups larger than 12, we recommend a 90-minute session.

How far in advance should we book?+

Four to six weeks before your travel dates, especially during peak season (December through April). Holiday weeks and spring break fill particularly fast.

Will you come to our resort?+

Yes. Our team travels to your resort or any location you choose — beach, pool terraces, tropical gardens, or architectural features. We also offer off-property sessions at cenotes, ruins, and private beaches.

How many edited photos will we receive?+

A 60-minute session yields 60-90 images. A 90-minute session produces 90-130. Every photograph is color-graded, retouched, and held to the same editorial standard.

What happens if it rains?+

Cancun's rain showers are typically brief (20-45 minutes) and often produce spectacular skies. If sustained rain is forecast, we reschedule at no additional cost. We monitor weather daily during your trip.

How long until we receive our gallery?+

A preview of 10-15 images is delivered within 72 hours. Your full curated gallery arrives within three to four weeks, with high-resolution print files and web-optimized versions.

Do you offer combined family and couples sessions?+

Yes. Many families add a 30-minute couples segment while grandparents enjoy time with the children. We also offer extended sessions with individual portraits of each child.
