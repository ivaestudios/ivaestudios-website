FAQ

Common questions before booking.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend 4-6 weeks before your travel dates, especially during peak season (November–April).

What time of day are sessions scheduled?

All sessions are at golden hour — the hour after sunrise or before sunset.

How many photos will we receive?

Fully edited, high-resolution images delivered within 1-3 business days to a private gallery.

What makes IVAE different from resort photographers?

We plan every session individually, shoot at golden hour, and deliver editorial-quality galleries.

Do you offer videography?

Yes — photography and videography, including destination wedding film packages.
