FAQ

Common questions.

We recommend booking 4 to 6 weeks before your travel dates, especially during peak season from November through April. Earlier booking ensures availability at your preferred resort and golden hour time slot.
All IVAE sessions are scheduled around golden hour — the hour after sunrise or the hour before sunset. This is when the light is softest and most cinematic, giving your images that editorial quality.
You will receive fully edited, high-resolution images delivered within 1 to 3 business days through a private online gallery. The exact number depends on your session length and package.
Resort photographers handle dozens of sessions per day with no advance planning. IVAE Studios plans every session individually, works exclusively during optimal light windows, and delivers editorial-quality galleries that look like they belong in a magazine.
