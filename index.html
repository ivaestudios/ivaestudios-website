Why IVAE

We're not a resort photographer. That's the point.

01

We photograph four families a month. Not forty.

Every session gets full pre-production — location scouting, wardrobe consult, timing around the light. You get our complete attention, not a slot between two other shoots.

02

Golden hour. Never midday.

We shoot the hour after sunrise or the hour before sunset — when the light is soft, the beaches are empty, and the color in your photos looks like the cover of a travel magazine.

03

Hand-graded. Not preset-dumped.

Your gallery is color-graded image by image — not batched through a Lightroom filter. The difference is the reason your friends will ask who shot it.

FAQ

Common questions.

We recommend booking 4 to 6 weeks before your travel dates, especially during peak season (November through April). Earlier booking ensures availability at your preferred resort and golden hour time slot.
Every IVAE session is scheduled around golden hour — the hour after sunrise or the hour before sunset. This is when the light is softest, the beaches are empty, and your images gain that editorial quality that sets them apart.
Your fully edited, high-resolution gallery is delivered within 1 to 3 business days through a private online gallery. Every image is hand-graded — no Lightroom preset batching.
Every trip is different, so we build a custom proposal once we know your destination, resort, and session length. Reach out through Instagram with your dates and we'll send a full quote within 24 hours.
