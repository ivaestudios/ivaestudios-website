The Studio

An editorial photography studio for luxury resorts in Mexico.

We build sessions for travelers who want photographs that look like a magazine spread, not a phone reel. Riviera Maya, Cancún, Los Cabos. Editorial direction, native bilingual.

Philosophy

How we think about photographs.

Four principles that decide what we shoot, what we don't, and how the gallery reads at the end.

  1. 01

    Direction over moments.

    Most resort photography waits for things to happen. We compose them. Light, posture, light again — every frame is decided.

  2. 02

    Restraint over volume.

    We don't deliver every frame. We hand-cull the strongest, color-graded together so the gallery reads as a single editorial set.

  3. 03

    Bilingual native, not translated.

    We work fluently in Spanish and English. Every direction in the language the moment requires.

  4. 04

    Resort-aware, not generic.

    Each resort has its own light, its own crowd, its own micro-geography. We scout. We adapt the session structure to where you're staying.

Process

How a session is built.

The five decisions on the homepage are the spine. Below is the part you don't usually see — the work that happens before the camera leaves the bag and after it returns.

Once a session is confirmed we send a one-page questionnaire: travel dates, resort and room number, who is in the photographs, the language each person prefers to be directed in, any wardrobe questions. It is short on purpose. The point is to know what we are walking into before we walk into it.

For sessions outside our regular resorts we scout. That can mean an in-person walkthrough or a remote read of the property using the resort's own maps and a working knowledge of the coastline. Either way we name two locations and a backup before the morning of the shoot, and we hold a weather contingency that doesn't waste your only golden hour.

On location we work to the light. Golden hour is not negotiable. Sessions begin roughly an hour before sunset (or just after sunrise on request); the structure inside that window is direction-led — composition, posture, eye-line — adjusting in real time as the light moves. Spanish or English, whichever fits the moment.

After the session we cull by hand. Not every frame is delivered: the strongest are selected and color-graded together so the gallery reads as one editorial set rather than a folder of shots. Skin tones stay warm and natural. Shadows hold their blue-green. The four light registers of the studio coexist on purpose.

Galleries are delivered private and password-protected, downloadable in print and web sizes. Turnaround is usually within two weeks for couples and family sessions, longer for weddings.

The studio

The studio, the gear, the gallery.

Base
Riviera Maya. Sessions across Mexico's three premium resort coastlines — Riviera Maya, Cancún and Los Cabos — without travel fees inside those regions. International or off-region requests are quoted separately.
Areas served
Riviera Maya · Cancún · Los Cabos. Resort-side editorial and the in-between — cenotes, marinas, private beach access, jungle paths.
Gear
We don't list our gear. The camera doesn't matter. The discipline does — the timing, the light, the way the frame is decided before it is taken.
Delivery
Galleries are private and password-protected, downloadable in print and web sizes. Hand-culled. Color-graded as a set. No watermarked previews, no upsell tiers.
Languages
English and Spanish, native fluency on both. Direction in the language the moment requires — sometimes mid-frame.
Pricing
Investment upon request. Each session is structured to the resort, the stay and the people in the photographs — there is no fixed tier.
