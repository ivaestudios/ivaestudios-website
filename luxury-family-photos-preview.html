The IVAE family experience

Not just photographs. A family archive.

An editorial documentary studio approach, designed for the way real families behave on vacation. Children stay children. Grandparents stay present. Parents stop performing.

Editorial · Bilingual · Golden hour

We do not direct families into shapes. We slow the day down, then watch carefully, and let the picture happen. The grandfather who never sits still. The toddler who buries her face in her mother's neck. The cousins who finally see each other after a year apart on opposite coasts.

Most resort photography is built around a single shot list, executed in fifteen minutes, between weddings, against the same hibiscus wall. Our sessions are built differently, around your family's pace, energy, and the last hour of light.

The result is a small, considered archive. Sixty to a hundred and thirty images that look like the cover of a travel issue, then quietly become the photographs your children show their children.

FAQ

Cancún family photographer questions.

Golden hour, the final 60 to 90 minutes before sunset, produces the most flattering, warm light. In Cancun, sunset ranges from approximately 5:45 PM in winter to 7:30 PM in summer. We schedule every session to coincide with this window.

We send a personalised wardrobe guide after booking. Coordinate around two or three complementary tones, ivory, sand, sage, dusty blue. Avoid neon colours, large logos, and busy patterns. Linen and flowing fabrics work best in ocean breezes.

We use a play-based approach. Kids explore and interact naturally while we capture genuine expressions. Sessions are planned around energy levels and nap schedules, with breaks for snacks. We never force poses.

Absolutely. We create a shot list that includes the full group, individual family units, grandparents with grandchildren, and sibling groups. For groups larger than 12, we recommend a 90 to 120 minute session.

Yes. Our team travels to your resort or any location you choose, beach, pool terraces, tropical gardens, or architectural features. We also offer off-property sessions at cenotes, ruins, and private beaches.

Cancun's rain showers are typically brief (20 to 45 minutes) and often produce spectacular skies. If sustained rain is forecast, we reschedule at no additional cost. We monitor weather daily during your trip.
