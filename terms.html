Terms of Service

The terms under which we work.

Last updated: May 2026.

Editorial draft. This is a first-draft policy written by IVAE Studios. We recommend reviewing with legal counsel before production launch.

1. Acceptance of terms

By visiting ivaestudios.com, sending us an inquiry, or booking a session, you agree to the terms below. If you cannot agree to them, please do not use the site or engage the studio. These terms govern the website and the studio's general practice; the specific session contract you sign at booking will govern the session itself, and where the two differ, the session contract prevails.

2. Services offered

IVAE Studios provides editorial-direction photography for families, couples, and destination weddings at luxury resorts in Riviera Maya, Cancún, and Los Cabos, together with bilingual production support and a private online gallery for image delivery. Pricing is provided on inquiry; the public site does not list session tiers.

3. Booking and cancellation

A session is confirmed when the studio has received both a signed session contract and the agreed deposit. The deposit reserves the date and is non-refundable; it covers the studio's preparation and the slot we hold for you. The remaining balance is due before the session begins.

If weather or a force-majeure event — hurricane warning, civil disruption, illness affecting the photographer or the client, or any other circumstance beyond reasonable control — prevents the session, the studio will rebook the session at no additional cost within a reasonable window of available dates. Where a rebook is not possible because the client has already left the country, the deposit converts into a credit valid for twelve months.

If the client cancels, the deposit is forfeit. Cancellation requested fewer than seven days before the session may also be invoiced for the balance, at the studio's discretion.

4. Use of the website

You may read the site, save pages for personal reference, and link to it. You may not scrape it, reverse-engineer the gallery infrastructure, train machine-learning models on its photographs, redistribute its images, or use them in any commercial context — your own or a third party's — without prior written permission from the studio. Photographs on this site are protected by Mexican copyright law (Ley Federal del Derecho de Autor) and by international copyright treaty.

5. Image rights

Image-rights terms are described in the Privacy Policy, section four — see Image rights and galleries. In short: photographs from your session are yours to use personally; the studio retains the right to use selected images for portfolio and marketing unless a private session contract is signed in advance. The session contract you sign at booking restates and refines these terms.

6. Limitation of liability

The site and the studio's services are provided as-is. To the fullest extent permitted by Mexican law and by the consumer-protection regime that applies to your purchase, the studio's total liability arising from a session — howsoever it arises — is limited to the fees you have paid for that session. The studio is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages, including loss of profit, loss of opportunity, or damages claimed by third parties present at the session. Nothing in this clause limits liability for fraud, gross negligence, or any liability that cannot be excluded under applicable law.

The competent courts of the State of Quintana Roo (or, where required for consumer matters, the venue specified by Mexican consumer-protection law) have jurisdiction over any dispute arising from these terms or from a session booked with the studio.

9. Contact and dispute resolution

If something goes wrong, the studio prefers to talk it through before anything else. Write to info@ivaestudios.com with a clear summary of the issue and we will respond within twenty working days, propose a remedy where one is appropriate, and try to reach a resolution without recourse to formal proceedings.
