Session Guide

What to Expect and What to Wear

Our team handles all of the planning and creative direction so you can focus entirely on each other. Here is how a typical couples session unfolds, along with our wardrobe recommendations for Mexico's landscape and light.

Before Your Session

After booking, you receive a detailed welcome guide covering your session timeline, location details, wardrobe suggestions tailored to your specific setting, and preparation tips. Our team confirms all logistics 48 hours in advance and monitors weather conditions to ensure optimal timing. You will know exactly where to be and when.

During the Session

Sessions begin with a few minutes of relaxed conversation to ease any camera nerves. From there, our team guides you through natural interactions — walking together, pausing to look at each other, whispering something real. There is no pose sheet. Direction is gentle, intuitive, and responsive to your energy. Most couples describe it as feeling like an elevated date rather than a formal photoshoot.

What to Wear

Neutral and earth tones photograph beautifully against Mexico's natural palette — whites, creams, soft beiges, sage greens, and muted blues. Flowy fabrics create gorgeous movement in beachside images. Avoid large logos, busy patterns, and neon colors. Our style guide includes specific recommendations for your destination, season, and session location.

After Your Session

A curated preview gallery of 10 to 15 images is typically delivered within 48 to 72 hours — often while you are still on vacation. Your complete professionally edited gallery of 50 to 120 images (depending on session length) arrives within two to three weeks via a private online gallery with full download and print-ordering capabilities.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Couples Photography in Mexico

How much does a couples photoshoot cost in Mexico?
Professional luxury couples sessions with IVAE Studios range from $450 to $1,200 USD depending on session duration, number of locations, and whether cinematic video is included. Most couples select a 90-minute golden-hour session at one or two locations, which delivers 80 to 120 professionally edited images. Contact our team for detailed pricing.
What is the best time of day for a couples photoshoot?
The golden hour — roughly the final 90 minutes before sunset — produces the most flattering, romantic light for couples photography in Mexico. This typically falls between 5:00 and 7:00 PM depending on the season. Sunrise sessions are equally beautiful and offer the advantage of deserted beaches and softer, cooler tones. Our team selects the optimal time based on your specific location and the time of year.
Can you photograph at our resort?
Yes. Our team works regularly at luxury resorts throughout Cancun, Riviera Maya and Los Cabos, including properties like Nizuc, Rosewood Mayakoba, Waldorf Astoria Los Cabos, Le Blanc, Hyatt Zilara, and many more. We handle all vendor coordination and access arrangements with your resort in advance so you never have to manage logistics.
How far in advance should we book a couples session?
We recommend booking at least four to six weeks before your travel dates, especially during peak season from November through April. For surprise proposals that require location scouting and coordination, eight weeks or more is ideal. Last-minute inquiries are always welcome and we accommodate them whenever our schedule allows.
What should we wear for a couples photoshoot in Mexico?
Neutral and earth tones photograph beautifully against Mexico's natural landscapes — whites, creams, soft beiges, sage greens, and muted blues. Flowy fabrics create gorgeous movement in beachside images. Avoid logos, busy patterns, and neon colors. After booking, our team provides a comprehensive style guide tailored to your specific session location and time of year.
Do you coordinate surprise proposals?
Absolutely. Proposal coordination is one of our signature services. Our team scouts the location, plans timing around optimal light, positions discreetly to capture the authentic reaction, and transitions into a celebratory portrait session immediately after. We also coordinate with resorts for floral arrangements, musicians, or private dining setups upon request.
How long until we receive our edited photos?
Your complete gallery of professionally edited images is delivered within two to three weeks via a private online gallery. A curated preview set of 10 to 15 images is typically shared within 48 to 72 hours of your session, so you can begin sharing highlights while you are still on your trip.
What if it rains on the day of our session?
Brief tropical rain showers are common in Mexico's Caribbean coast, especially between June and October, but they typically pass within 30 to 60 minutes. Our team monitors weather patterns closely and adjusts session timing in real time. The moments immediately after rain often produce the most dramatic skies and light of the day. If sustained weather prevents the session entirely, we reschedule at no additional cost.
Can we combine a couples session with other photography services?
Yes. Many of our clients pair a couples session with a maternity session, family portrait session, or event coverage during the same trip. Combination packages are available and offer both scheduling convenience and preferred pricing. Our team is happy to design a custom itinerary that covers everything you need during your stay.
