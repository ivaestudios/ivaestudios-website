FAQ

Questions from hotel marketing teams.

Do you manage social media for luxury hotels in Cancun and Riviera Maya?
Yes. The studio specializes in luxury hospitality. We work with boutique hotels and resorts across Cancun, Riviera Maya, Tulum and Los Cabos, building editorial Instagram and TikTok presences that match the property's design language.
What does a hotel social media plan include?
Each plan includes on-property photo and video sessions, an editorial calendar, multi-channel publishing, bilingual community management and a monthly report. Plans scale from one session per month to weekly sessions and paid campaigns.
Can you produce content in both English and Spanish?
Yes. The team is bilingual. Captions, stories and direct messages are handled in both English and Spanish so the brand reaches domestic and international guests.
Do you handle paid campaigns for hotels?
Yes. The Enterprise plan includes paid social campaigns on Instagram and Meta, with creative production, targeting and reporting aligned to revenue goals like direct bookings and group inquiries.
How fast can a luxury hotel see results?
Visual consistency arrives in month one. Engagement and follower lift typically show in months two and three. Bookings attributable to social are tracked from month three using UTM links and direct inquiries.
Do you sign annual contracts?
No forced annual contracts. The studio works with flexible monthly plans and a recommended three-month minimum to give the editorial strategy time to compound.
