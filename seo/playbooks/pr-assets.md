# Manual PR Pitch Playbook — 5 Pitch-able Assets

Each asset below is something IVAE Studios is uniquely positioned to publish.
This is the owner's outreach list — the agent does not auto-pitch (legal +
spam risk). Status field is meant to be edited inline as pitches progress.

---

## 1. Cenote Underwater Photography Guide

- **Asset:** Long-form guide to shooting cenotes (light angles, gear, permits,
  best months, ideal cenotes by experience level).
- **Target publications:** Vogue (Living section), Travel + Leisure (Mexico
  vertical), Condé Nast Traveler.
- **Pitch angle:** "How to plan a once-in-a-lifetime underwater portrait
  session in the Yucatán cenotes — by the photographer luxury resorts call."
- **Outreach contact pattern:** travel editor or features editor; pitch via
  the publication's general pitch email or Muck Rack profile. Bcc PR contact
  if the resort partner allows.
- **Status:** Idea

---

## 2. 2026 Mexico Destination Wedding Cost Report

- **Asset:** Data-backed report on what 2026 destination weddings actually
  cost in Cancún / Riviera Maya / Los Cabos (venue, photo, planner, travel,
  tax). Sourced from real client invoices (anonymised).
- **Target publications:** The Knot (planning vertical; nota: la dueña evaluó
  The Knot de PAGA como mala inversión — solo pitch editorial gratuito, no
  comprar placement), Brides.com (real weddings + planning), Martha Stewart
  Weddings.
- **Pitch angle:** "First-of-its-kind transparent pricing report for 2026
  Mexico destination weddings — with median cost by venue tier."
- **Outreach contact pattern:** wedding-features editor; co-pitch with one
  named planner partner for credibility. Always include the report PDF
  upfront — never gate behind email signup for press.
- **Status:** Idea

---

## 3. "Resort Photographer vs Editorial Photographer" Comparison

- **Asset:** Honest decision guide explaining the difference between in-house
  resort photographers, freelance budget shooters, and editorial studios —
  without naming competitors.
- **Target publications:** Hello! (luxury vertical), Harper's Bazaar (travel),
  Town & Country.
- **Pitch angle:** "Most travellers don't realise the photo package included
  with their resort booking is a different product to an editorial shoot —
  here's the framework for choosing."
- **Outreach contact pattern:** lifestyle or travel editor. Lead with the
  framework, not the studio. Offer Vianey Díaz as a quotable expert.
- **Status:** Idea

---

## 4. Golden-Hour Calendar Cancún 2026

- **Asset:** Interactive PR-able 12-month calendar showing exact golden-hour
  windows for Cancún, Tulum, and Cabo — with month-by-month styling notes.
  Embeddable iframe + downloadable PDF.
- **Target publications:** travel blogs (Nomadic Matt, The Blonde Abroad),
  destination wedding planners' blogs, Pinterest creators.
- **Pitch angle:** "Stop guessing. Here is every golden-hour window in Cancún
  for 2026, plus what to wear in each month — free embed for your blog."
- **Outreach contact pattern:** blogger or creator outreach; offer the embed
  with attribution back to ivaestudios.com. Never ask for backlink — the
  embed credit is the link.
- **Status:** Idea

---

## 5. "Best 2026 Mexico Wedding Destinations" Report

- **Asset:** Ranked list of 12 Mexican wedding destinations for 2026 with
  travel-cost trends, weather, accessibility, and photo-friendliness scores.
- **Target publications:** Destination Weddings & Honeymoons magazine,
  Wedding Sutra, Junebug Weddings.
- **Pitch angle:** "We've shot 200+ destination weddings across Mexico —
  here's the data-driven 2026 destination ranking your readers won't find
  anywhere else."
- **Outreach contact pattern:** features editor at each publication; offer
  exclusive top-3 reveal to the first publication that responds within 7
  days, then publish in full on ivaestudios.com.
- **Status:** Idea

---

## How to use this list

1. Pick one asset per quarter (don't pitch all five in the same month — fatigue).
2. Update the **Status** field inline: `Idea → Drafted → Pitched → Live`.
3. After publication, add a row to `seo/reports/` and submit the new live URL
   via `python3 scripts/index_urls.py <URL>` for fast Google indexing.
4. Save responses (positive AND no-response) — fold into the next quarter's
   pitch revisions.
