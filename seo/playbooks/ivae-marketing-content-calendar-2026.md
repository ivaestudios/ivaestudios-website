## Engagement frameworks

### Cada blog post incluye

- 5-section structure: Hook, Context, Framework, Examples, CTA
- 1 pull-quote en medio (320px Cormorant ❝)
- 6-question FAQ al final (con FAQPage schema)
- 3 internal links (a master page, a landing industria, a otro blog)
- 2 external links (a fuente autorizada + a entidad mencionada)
- Author byline: "By the IVAE Marketing studio team"

### Cada LinkedIn post incluye

- Hook en línea 1 (que aparece en feed sin "ver más")
- Story/insight/value en líneas 2-7
- CTA suave en línea 8 (no sales-y)
- Hashtag pack: 3-5 hashtags relevantes (no 30)

### Cada Reel incluye

- Hook visual en primeros 0.5s
- Soundtrack curado (no trending viral — trending editorial)
- Texto overlay máximo 5 palabras
- CTA en caption

## Editorial principles (voice rules)

**Tone:** Editorial, restrained, never sales-y. Confident, not arrogant.

**Voice elements:**
- "The studio" / "We" (NOT "I" personal de Vianey)
- "Editorial discipline"
- "Bilingual native"
- "Golden-hour only"

**Avoid:**
- Em-dashes (—) in body text
- "Capture moments"
- "Engagement boost"
- "Viral content"
- "Trendy"
- "Game-changing"
- "Synergy"

**Cadence:**
- Short sentences. Editorial.
- Mix with longer, contextual ones that build the picture.
- Periods. Commas. Sometimes slashes / for nested ideas.
