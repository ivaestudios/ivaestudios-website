=== Founding ===
IVAE Marketing was launched in 2024 as an extension of [[IVAE Studios]]. Founder Vianey Díaz extended the photography studio's editorial workflow into social media management to address what she described as a "production-distribution gap" common in hotel marketing, where photo agencies and social agencies operate separately.<ref name="founding"/>

## Submission process

### Step 1: Establish notability (pre-submit)

Wait until 3+ independent sources exist before submitting. Submitting early = likely rejection = harder to resubmit later.

### Step 2: Create draft

Use [[Wikipedia:Articles for creation]]:
- URL: `https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Articles_for_creation`
- Spanish: `https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Asistente_para_la_creaci%C3%B3n_de_art%C3%ADculos`

### Step 3: Submit

- Include ALL sources as `<ref>` tags
- Use neutral, encyclopedic tone (NO marketing language)
- Avoid all promotional language ("the best", "leading", "industry-first", etc.)
- Include criticisms or limitations if any exist publicly

### Step 4: Wait

AfC review typically takes 6-12 weeks. Be patient.

### Step 5: Respond to reviewer feedback

If declined, address each point and resubmit. Most articles need 2-3 cycles.

## Tone guidelines (per Wikipedia)

**FORBIDDEN:**
- "The best", "leading", "premier", "industry-first"
- "Beautiful", "stunning", "incredible"
- "Founded by visionary"
- Any superlatives without source
- Marketing copy
- Em-dashes

**REQUIRED:**
- Neutral point of view
- Verifiable facts
- Inline citations for every claim
- Past tense for history
- Present tense for ongoing operations
