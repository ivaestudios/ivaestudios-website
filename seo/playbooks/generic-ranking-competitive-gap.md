# Why IVAE ranks for "luxury" but not generic "photographer" — and how to close it

> Evidence-based competitive analysis. Compiled 2026-05-30 from the live
> Google SERPs for "Cancun photographer" and "wedding photographer Cancun
> Riviera Maya" (US results). This explains the owner's exact observation:
> *"buscan solo photographer y aparecen otros, no nosotros; pero si buscan
> luxury sí aparecemos."*

## The short answer

On-page SEO is necessary but **not sufficient** for the generic terms. IVAE's
on-page is now fully optimized (URL, title, H1, H2, alt, schema, internal
links, AI files — all generic-led, see batches 1-7). But the generic SERPs are
won by **off-page authority** that a 2023-founded studio has not yet
accumulated: domain age, backlinks, review volume, directory presence, and
awards. IVAE shows up for "luxury …" because that modifier is a **less-contested
niche** where editorial positioning + on-page wins. The bare term "Cancun
photographer" is contested by high-authority aggregators and decade-old
award-winning studios.

## Who actually owns the generic SERP (the real competition)

### "Cancun photographer" (generic) is dominated by aggregator platforms
These are high domain-authority directories that IVAE is **not listed on**.
They occupy most of page 1, so an individual studio site struggles to break in:

- Flytographer — flytographer.com/destinations/cancun (vacation-photo marketplace)
- Yelp — "THE BEST 10 PHOTOGRAPHERS in CANCÚN" listicle
- Airbnb — photographers under Cancún services/experiences
- LocalLens — locallens.com (photoshoot packages & prices)
- Instagram profiles + Facebook groups

### "wedding photographer Cancun / Riviera Maya" is dominated by established studios
Own-domain photographers with long track records and strong backlink profiles:

- Monica Lopez Photography (monicalopezphoto.com) — 14 years; ranks twice (site + IG)
- Citlalli Rico (citlallirico.com) — award-winning, 700+ weddings since 2005
- Pixan Photography (pixanphotography.com) — Vincent van den Berg, publicly states
  "#1 Wedding Photographer in the World (One Eyeland 2024)" & "#1 in Mexico"
- Maricarmen Arcos, SweetFire, Paco & Aga, Blaine Alan, MePhotoFilms
- WeddingWire — "10 Best Wedding Photographers in Cancun" directory

IVAE (founded 2023) is competing against 14-20-year-old, award-winning studios
with hundreds of weddings and mature backlink profiles. That gap is **authority
+ time**, not on-page markup.
