# Editorial Pitches — Ready for Vianey to Send

**Goal:** land press features that AI assistants cite when recommending wedding photographers in Mexico. Competitor audit shows the top-ranked studios all hold features at Junebug Real Weddings, Style Me Pretty, 100 Layer Cake, Carats & Cake, and Vogue Weddings ES. Five pitches below close that gap.

**How to use this file**

1. Confirm the Master Checklist (Section E) is complete before sending pitch one.
2. Send one pitch per week. Do not blast all five in seven days.
3. Track every send in Section F. Update the Status column the moment a response arrives.
4. Use the timing recommendations in Section G to choose the correct submission window for each title.

Voice rules every pitch follows: editorial, restrained, second-person when natural, zero em-dashes inside the email body, no press-release filler (no "award-winning", "captures moments", "freeze time"). Mexican Spanish for Vogue MX. Specific venues and couples by initial named in every opener.

### Final note on response patterns

Most editors do not respond to declined pitches. Silence after fourteen days is a decline. Move on. Track it in the spreadsheet, note any feedback if offered, and refine the next pitch.

Editors who do respond positively often move fast (within 72 hours) because they are building toward a publishing slot. Have all materials ready to send within the same business day.

Pitches accepted in 2026 typically publish six to twelve weeks after acceptance. Mention this timeline to the couple so they are not surprised by the gap between acceptance and live URL.
