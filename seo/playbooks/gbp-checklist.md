# Google Business Profile — Weekly Checklist

GBP signals are out-of-repo: they live in the Google Business Profile dashboard,
not in the codebase. This checklist is for the owner (Vianey) to run every
Monday. Treat each `[ ]` as a real action item.

## Every week (15 min)

- [ ] **Post 1 photo** — drop one golden-hour shot from the most recent
  session. Caption with location ("Cancún", "Riviera Maya", "Los Cabos") and
  one keyword phrase.
- [ ] **Post 1 update** — booking-availability note ("November 2026 dates
  open"), seasonal note, or behind-the-scenes line. Even 80 chars is fine.
- [ ] **Reply to all reviews within 48h** — including 5-star ones. Address
  the reviewer by first name; mention the location of their shoot.
- [ ] **Q&A: seed + answer** — if total Q&A on the profile < 10, post one new
  brand-safe question + answer (e.g. "Is IVAE Studios open weekends?",
  "Do you photograph in cenotes?"). Use the customer-question UI; answer in
  the owner-response UI.
- [ ] **Photo cap check** — confirm 50+ photos on profile. Geo-tag any new
  photos before upload (use the iPhone Photos → Info pane to set GPS, or
  Lightroom → Map module).
- [ ] **Attributes check** — verify the following are still toggled on:
  *Online appointments*, *LGBTQ+ friendly*, *Wheelchair accessible parking*.
  Add seasonal attributes when relevant.

## Monthly (~30 min, first Monday)

- [ ] **Service list audit** — open Services tab, confirm all 8 service
  packages match the website prices and descriptions.
- [ ] **Hours sanity check** — adjust for any upcoming Mexican public
  holidays or planned travel.
- [ ] **Insights screenshot** — download the last 28 days insights as PNG
  and store in your private archive folder. Used during quarterly review.

## Quarterly (~60 min, first Monday of month 1, 4, 7, 10)

- [ ] **Photo refresh** — replace the 5 oldest profile photos with newer
  hero-grade work. Old photos can hurt Discover impressions.
- [ ] **Posts archive review** — check that recent posts still have valid
  CTAs (no expired offers, no broken booking links).
- [ ] **Cross-platform consistency** — confirm phone, address, hours match
  what's in `index.html` JSON-LD `Organization` schema, and what's on
  Instagram bio + Pinterest profile.
