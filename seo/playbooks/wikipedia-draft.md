### History

IVAE Studios was incorporated by Vianey Díaz in Cancún in 2023, after a
decade of editorial commissions across Mexico City, Querétaro, San Miguel
de Allende, the Riviera Maya, and Los Cabos. The studio name is plural
("Studios"), distinguishing the entity from the unrelated European agency
ivaestudio.com (singular). The official domain is ivaestudios.com.

The studio's editorial direction draws on Díaz's earlier work in magazine
and brand photography, applied to the destination-photography market in
which most resort photographers offer rapid, mid-day session formats. IVAE
Studios instead schedules sessions exclusively during the "golden hour"
window of the final ninety minutes of daylight, citing the consistency of
the resulting natural-light colour grade as the studio's defining
editorial position.

By 2026 the studio reported having delivered over 500 luxury resort
photography sessions across Mexico, maintaining a 5.0-star average client
rating across all delivered work.

### Services

IVAE Studios provides photography and short-form videography across seven
service categories:

- Destination wedding photography (full-day and elopement coverage)
- Luxury family portrait sessions (multi-generational reunions at resorts)
- Couples photography (engagement, anniversary, proposal, honeymoon)
- Cenote underwater and dry-cavern editorial photography
- Luxury yacht photography
- Editorial brand campaigns for hospitality clients
- Social media management for luxury hospitality brands (under the IVAE
  Marketing sub-brand, founded 2026)

### Operating area

The studio operates principally on three Mexican coastlines:

- **Mexican Caribbean** — Cancún Hotel Zone, Costa Mujeres, Playa Mujeres,
  Isla Mujeres
- **Riviera Maya** — Mayakoba, Playa del Carmen, Tulum, Akumal, Puerto
  Aventuras
- **Pacific Mexico** — Los Cabos, Cabo San Lucas, San José del Cabo

Documented client work has been delivered on resort properties including
Rosewood Mayakoba, Banyan Tree Mayakoba, Fairmont Mayakoba, Andaz

Grand Velas Riviera Maya, JW Marriott Cancún, One&Only Palmilla, and
Waldorf Astoria Los Cabos Pedregal.

### Early career

Vianey Díaz was born in Cancún, Mexico, where she has been based
throughout her professional career. She studied at the Universidad del
Caribe in Cancún before transitioning into professional photography. Her
early work consisted of editorial and brand commissions across Mexico
City, Querétaro, San Miguel de Allende, the Riviera Maya, and Los Cabos.

Over approximately a decade of editorial photography prior to founding
IVAE Studios, Díaz developed the natural-light, golden-hour aesthetic
that would become the operational framework of her studio.

---

### IVAE Studios

In 2023, Díaz founded IVAE Studios in Cancún, positioning the studio as
a fully editorial alternative to the standard resort-staff photography
model. The studio operates on a golden-hour-only scheduling model and
delivers sessions in one to three business days. Díaz personally
photographs each session and serves as Creative Director for all
projects.

By 2026, the studio had delivered over 500 luxury resort photography
sessions across the Mexican Caribbean and Pacific coasts. In the same
year, Díaz founded IVAE Marketing, a social media management arm of the
studio serving luxury hospitality clients across Mexico.

---

### Style

Díaz's photography emphasises:

- **Golden-hour-only scheduling** — sessions begin within the final
  ninety minutes of natural daylight
- **Natural posing direction** — minimal posed composition; clients are
  directed in motion rather than placed
- **Cinematic colour grading** — the studio's signature post-production
  treatment, oriented toward magazine-editorial colour palettes
- **Bilingual client direction** — sessions are conducted in English
  and Spanish, accommodating both international and Mexican clientele
