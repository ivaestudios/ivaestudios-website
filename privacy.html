Privacy Policy

How we handle your information.

Last updated: May 2026.

Editorial draft. This is a first-draft policy written by IVAE Studios. We recommend reviewing with legal counsel before production launch.

1. What we collect

When you contact the studio we keep what you send us — typically a name, an email address, a phone number, your travel dates, the resort or destination you have in mind, and any context you share about the session. None of this is required to read the site; we only collect it once you choose to write to us.

On the site itself we keep standard server logs (IP address, user-agent, request paths, referring page) for analytics, caching, and abuse prevention. We do not run third-party advertising pixels, cross-site identity graphs, or behavioural retargeting. The site is not built that way.

2. How we use it

We use the information you send us to respond to your inquiry, to coordinate the session — schedule, location, briefing — and, after the shoot, to deliver your gallery. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. We do not share your contact details with planners, vendors, or resorts unless you ask us to and we confirm it in writing.

3. Cookies and analytics

The marketing site uses minimal first-party cookies — language preference, the small flags that keep navigation consistent across pages. The client gallery (when applicable) sets a session cookie so you remain signed in while you review your photographs.

For traffic analytics we use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is privacy-respecting by design — it does not track individuals, does not fingerprint browsers, and does not use third-party cookies. We do not run Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or similar tools on this site.

4. Image rights and galleries

The photographs from your session are yours to use personally — printing, framing, sharing with family, posting to your own social accounts. They are delivered through a private gallery link with a passphrase you control.

By default, the studio retains the right to use selected images from your session for portfolio, editorial, and marketing purposes — the website, Instagram, journal pieces, print samples. If you prefer your session to remain entirely private, request a private session contract before booking and we will replace these defaults with explicit non-publication terms.

We do not sell images of you to third parties under any circumstance.

5. Data retention

Inquiries that do not become bookings are kept for up to 24 months and then deleted. We hold them this long because travel plans are slow — guests sometimes return to a thread the following year.

Once we have a session relationship with you we keep your contact details, the session brief, the contract, and the invoice for as long as is reasonable for accounting and customer-service purposes. Gallery image files remain on private storage for 12 months after delivery, then move to encrypted offline archive. You can ask us to remove the live gallery sooner.

6. Your rights

If you live in Mexico, the European Union, the United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with comparable rules — México's Federal Law on the Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties (LFPDPPP), the GDPR, and similar — you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to correct it if it is wrong, to ask us to delete it, to oppose certain processing, and to receive a copy in a portable format.

To exercise any of these, write to info@ivaestudios.com from the address you originally used. We aim to respond within twenty working days.

7. Cross-border transfers

IVAE Studios is based in México. Some of the services we use to operate the studio — email hosting, website hosting, gallery storage, transactional infrastructure — sit on servers in the United States and Europe. Where personal data crosses a border we rely on standard data-transfer safeguards (the contractual clauses our processors offer; equivalent-protection commitments) and we keep the list of processors short and intentional.

8. Security

Personal data we hold on your behalf is encrypted in transit, and at rest where the storage layer supports it. Access to inquiries, contracts, and galleries is limited to people working on your session.

No system is perfectly secure. If we discover a breach that affects you, we will tell you within seventy-two hours of becoming aware of it, in line with the breach-notification standard set by the GDPR — even where local law would not require us to.

9. Changes

If we change this policy we post the revised text on this page and update the "last updated" date at the top. If a change is material — narrowing your rights, broadening our use of your data — we will tell you by email if we have one for you.

10. Contact

Privacy questions, deletion requests, complaints: info@ivaestudios.com. The studio is reachable Monday through Saturday; please allow up to twenty working days for a substantive reply.

This document was drafted in English; the Spanish version is provided for convenience. In case of conflict, the English version prevails.
