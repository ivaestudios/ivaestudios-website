Wedding Day Guide

Designed around the light.

12:00

Getting Ready

Hair, makeup, bridal details. Dress, shoes, rings, invitation suite, moments of anticipation.

2:00

First Look

Private first look before the ceremony. 20-30 minutes of relaxed couple portraits in beautiful afternoon light.

3:30

Pre-Ceremony

Ceremony details, venue atmosphere, guest arrivals. Warm, directional light ideal for documentary images.

4:00

Ceremony

Scheduled to finish 90 minutes before sunset, giving us the full golden hour arc for portraits.

5:15

Golden Hour Portraits

The most magical window. Warm amber light across the beach or architecture. This is when iconic images are created.

5:45

Sunset & Cocktails

Dramatic silhouettes transition into cocktail hour. Guest candids as the sky transforms.

7:00

Reception

Grand entrance, first dance, toasts, dinner, dancing. Refined lighting that maintains ambiance.
