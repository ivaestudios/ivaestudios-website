The Mexican dental landscape in 2026.

If you are weighing whether to invest in social media for your clinic, three numbers matter more than the rest.

01

3x growth since 2018

Mexican dental tourism has tripled, with Cancun, Tijuana, Merida, Monterrey and Los Algodones as the highest-volume corridors in the Americas. Roughly 1.4 million US patients now cross annually for dental care.

02

35-60% discovery on TikTok

For cosmetic dentistry, full-mouth restoration and dental tourism, TikTok now drives 35 to 60 percent of new patient awareness. Clinics on Instagram alone are discovered at half the rate of competitors running both.

03

40-60% leak rate

Most Mexican dental clinics leak 40 to 60 percent of qualified inbound DMs because the response is "please call our office." The fix is a structured DM playbook, not a content fix.

FAQ

Questions from clinic directors.

Do you do marketing for dental clinics in Mexico?
Yes. The studio runs social media for dental clinics across Mexico, with a focus on cosmetic dentistry, implants, veneers, full-mouth restoration and dental tourism.
How do you handle patient privacy and LFPDPPP compliance?
All patient-facing content runs through a written, tiered consent workflow that complies with Mexico's LFPDPPP. Faces, names and identifying details are only used with signed releases. Three consent tiers are documented per patient (Tier A full face, Tier B lower face, Tier C intra-oral only). Before-and-after content respects Mexican medical advertising standards.
Can you target US and Canadian patients for dental tourism?
Yes. Funnels are built bilingually for US and Canadian patients evaluating dental tourism in Cancun, Los Algodones, Tijuana, Merida and Monterrey. Captions, ads and DMs run in English alongside Spanish content for local patients. Roughly 1.4 million US patients now cross annually for dental care in Mexico.
Do you produce patient testimonial videos?
Yes. Patient testimonial videos are a core asset. We brief, film and edit testimonials on site, with consent, and adapt them for Reels, TikTok and YouTube Shorts. Video testimonials outperform written reviews by 4 to 8 times on inquiry conversion in 2026.
Do you run lead-generation ads for dental clinics?
Yes. The Enterprise plan includes paid Instagram, TikTok and Meta campaigns built around consultation requests, with intake automation handing leads to the clinic CRM. The 2026 baseline budget for single-location cosmetic clinics is 1,200 to 3,000 USD per month for paid; dental tourism clinics scale to 3,500 to 9,000 USD.
Can you work with multi-location dental groups?
Yes. The Enterprise plan supports multi-clinic operations with location-aware content, geo-targeted ads and per-clinic reporting.
How is IVAE Marketing different from a generalist agency or an in-house hire?
IVAE Marketing is a photographer-led editorial studio that specializes in luxury hospitality and clinical brands. A generalist agency typically applies the same playbook to a restaurant and a dental clinic. An in-house junior hire ($12K-$18K USD/year in Cancun) has no backup and limited LFPDPPP fluency. IVAE Marketing provides editorial-grade production, structured DM playbook, bilingual reach and compliance from day one.
What can a dental clinic expect in the first 90 days?
Month 1 is foundation: audit, consent workflow, first production sprint. Month 2 is organic momentum: 20+ posts live, daily stories, DM-to-CRM intake, measurable inquiry-volume lift. Month 3 is paid amplification: Meta plus TikTok campaigns, cost-per-lead baseline, first post-treatment case study. Meaningful booked-patient uplift typically lands at the 90-120 day mark.
