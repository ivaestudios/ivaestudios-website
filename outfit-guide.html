Quick Reference

What to Wear vs. What to Avoid

A visual cheat sheet to keep your outfit session-ready.

Wear

Neutral & earth tones — cream, beige, sage, dusty rose, terracotta, navy
Lightweight fabrics — linen, cotton, chiffon, silk
Flowing silhouettes — maxi dresses, linen shirts, relaxed fits
Barefoot or simple sandals — authentic and universally flattering
Delicate jewelry — thin gold chains, subtle earrings

Avoid

Neon & bright saturated colors — they cast unnatural tints on skin
Large logos & graphic prints — they date photos and create visual clutter
All-black outfits — absorb light and appear as flat voids on the beach
Heavy fabrics — polyester, thick denim, wool trap heat and restrict movement
Busy small patterns — create moire effects and compete for attention
