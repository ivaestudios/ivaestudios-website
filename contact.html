Common questions about the inquiry.

What happens after I send this?

You receive a personal reply within 24 hours from Vianey or the studio inbox. We respond with availability for your dates, an estimated session structure, and any clarifying questions about your resort or location. If we're a fit, we send a quote and a hold-the-date arrangement.

Am I committed by sending this form?

No. The inquiry is just that — an inquiry. Nothing is held until we mutually agree on dates and you confirm.

What languages do you respond in?

English or Spanish, depending on what you wrote in. Both are native.
