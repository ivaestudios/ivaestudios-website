The work, in her words

"I do not photograph fifty families a week. I do not deliver galleries in six weeks. I work the final ninety minutes of daylight, in the hour when Mexican light becomes honest, and I deliver in one to three days. That is the studio. That is the discipline."

Born and raised in Cancún, Vianey speaks the language of two markets at once. International couples flying in for the destination, and Mexican families who know the city like a neighborhood. The studio's editorial visual direction draws from a decade of magazine and brand commissions across Mexico City, Querétaro, San Miguel de Allende, the Riviera Maya, and Los Cabos.

What she specializes in

  • Destination wedding photography. Full-day or elopement coverage at Mexico's finest resorts.
  • Luxury family portrait sessions. Multi-generational reunions at resort properties.
  • Couples photography. Engagements, anniversaries, proposals, honeymoons.
  • Cenote underwater & dry-cavern editorial. Tulum, Riviera Maya.
  • Luxury yacht photography. Isla Mujeres, Cancún.
  • Editorial brand & hotel campaigns. Magazine-quality visual direction for hospitality clients.

Where she works

IVAE Studios serves three coastlines. The Mexican Caribbean. Cancún Hotel Zone, Costa Mujeres, Playa Mujeres, Isla Mujeres, Riviera Maya, Mayakoba, Tulum, Akumal, Playa del Carmen. The lagoon-resort archipelago of Mayakoba. And the Pacific. Los Cabos, Cabo San Lucas, San José del Cabo. The studio is built around the editorial discipline that gives every coastline its own voice.
