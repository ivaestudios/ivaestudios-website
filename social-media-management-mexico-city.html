The CDMX brief

A social media agency built for Mexico City.

Mexico City is one of the most competitive hospitality and lifestyle markets in Latin America. A boutique hotel in Polanco, a fine dining concept in Roma and an aesthetic clinic in Lomas are all fighting for the same attention on the same feeds, often against well funded in-house teams. In that environment, a social media agency in Mexico City cannot just schedule posts. It has to produce a visual identity strong enough to stand out in the scroll, and do it consistently, month after month.

That is the gap IVAE Marketing was built to fill. We are the social media sub-brand of IVAE Studios, a luxury editorial photography studio, so every account starts from original photography and video instead of stock libraries or recycled templates. The same eye that shoots magazine-grade hospitality imagery in Cancun directs the content calendar for our CDMX clients. The result is a feed that reads as a brand, with reservations and inquiries to match.

Our team produces on the ground in Mexico City and runs Instagram, TikTok and Facebook end to end: editorial calendar, captions written to convert in English and Spanish, stories, Reels, carousels and monthly reporting on reach, engagement and growth. For brands that also operate in the Caribbean, we run CDMX and Cancun under one retainer with a single calendar, so the visual language stays coherent across both markets.

FAQ

Questions about social media in CDMX.

Does IVAE Marketing work in Mexico City?
Yes. Mexico City (CDMX) is one of IVAE Marketing's two core markets, alongside Cancun. The team produces on-site sessions in CDMX and runs the channels of hospitality brands, restaurants, clinics and premium developments with the same editorial discipline the studio brings to luxury photography.
Which areas of Mexico City do you cover?
We work with brands in Polanco, Roma, Condesa, Reforma, Santa Fe, Lomas, Del Valle and the Coyoacan and San Angel area. Production is scheduled in blocks to optimize travel across the city.
Do you handle both Cancun and Mexico City?
Yes. IVAE Marketing operates primarily in Cancun and CDMX. For groups with a presence in both markets, we coordinate a single retainer with production in both cities and a unified content calendar per brand.
What kind of businesses do you work with in CDMX?
Boutique hotels, restaurants and fine dining, aesthetic and dental clinics, spa and wellness, real estate developments and premium brands. The aesthetic is calibrated per brand, with editorial restraint for luxury and warmth for more casual concepts.
What is included in the social media service in Mexico City?
On-site content production (photo and vertical video), full management of Instagram, TikTok and Facebook, bilingual community management, an editorial calendar and monthly reports. Paid campaigns and creator coordination are included in the Signature plan.
Why choose an agency run by an editorial photography studio?
Because the feed looks like a magazine, not a marketing calendar. IVAE Marketing is the only social media agency in Mexico run by a luxury editorial photography studio, so every image in the feed is produced by an editorial team instead of assembled from stock or templates.
