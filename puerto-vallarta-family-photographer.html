The Session

A family photographer for the Pacific coast

As your Puerto Vallarta family photographer, our studio works the stretch of Mexican coast where the Sierra Madre jungle tumbles straight down to Banderas Bay. The town is golden sand and cobbled streets, bougainvillea-draped facades and a Malecón boardwalk that follows the water, all under a sun that sets directly over the Pacific. We build every family session around that hour, in the Zona Romántica, on the south-shore coves, and across the bay in Nuevo Vallarta and Sayulita, so your portraits carry the specific warmth and color of Vallarta rather than a postcard that could be anywhere.

A note on honesty, because it matters: IVAE Studios is based in Cancún, on the Caribbean side of Mexico, and we travel to Puerto Vallarta for family sessions. We do not keep a local Vallarta office. What you book is the same editorial studio, the same bilingual direction and the same warm, luminous gallery we bring to the Caribbean, simply applied to the Pacific. Travel is folded into your quote, and we plan the logistics and the golden-hour timing around Banderas Bay in advance.

The work is editorial and unhurried. We direct gently, in English or Spanish, and we read the room: a two-year-old who needs to run into the surf at Los Muertos, a grandfather who would rather sit on the warm sand than stand, a teenager who relaxes the moment nobody asks them to smile. We pace the session so each generation stays comfortable through the golden-hour window, then move quietly between two or three nearby setups. The result is a family that looks like itself, framed against a coastline that looks like nowhere else.

This page is for families specifically. If you are planning a wedding, a proposal, or a couples session on the Pacific, our broader couples photography and destination wedding pages cover those. And our wider destination family photography service spans both Mexican coastlines under one studio.

Where We Shoot

Family photo spots around Banderas Bay

01

Playa Los Muertos

The wide, walkable main beach of the Zona Romántica, with the colorful Pier of the Hanging Men in frame. Soft golden sand for little ones to play, easy access from town, and the easiest place to catch the sun setting straight into the bay.

02

Conchas Chinas Coves

Just south of town, the sheltered coves of Conchas Chinas offer calmer water, tide pools, and dramatic rock formations. The signature setting for relaxed, in-the-water family sequences away from the busier beaches.

03

The Malecón & Old Town

The seafront boardwalk with its bronze sculptures, plus the cobbled streets and bougainvillea of the Romantic Zone, give the bay a colorful, walkable texture. A graceful change of pace from the open beach and a strong rain-day fallback.

04

Nuevo Vallarta & Flamingos

North across the bay, the long, flat, resort-lined beaches are gentle for young children and easy for large groups. Wide-open sand and clear horizons make this a favorite for multi-generation reunions and big families.

05

Sayulita & the Riviera Nayarit

A little further up the coast, Sayulita brings jungle, surf, and a bohemian, colorful old town for families who want a barefoot, relaxed feel. Bucerías and Punta de Mita add quieter village beaches and calm coves at the mouth of the bay.

06

Your Resort

The beaches and grounds of Banderas Bay resorts, from the Hotel Zone to Punta de Mita, photograph beautifully: palm walkways, infinity edges, and golden sand, all coordinated with your concierge around your dinner reservation.

Light & Timing

How the light moves over the bay

Sunset over the water

Banderas Bay faces west, so the sun sets directly over the Pacific, wrapping families in warm gold as it falls toward the horizon. We meet you about ninety minutes before sunset and shoot through the soft window as the bay calms and the sky turns.

Sunrise for early risers

Families with young, early-waking children often do best at sunrise: near-empty beaches, gentle rose light, and cooler sand. We use it for the calmest sessions and for the quietest frames in the old town before the Malecón fills with the day.

Dry season vs. green season

November through April brings the most reliable, comfortable weather and is peak season for visiting families. Summer and early autumn are lush, green, and humid with afternoon rain, so we time sessions carefully and keep a flexible, weather-aware plan.

Tides & crowds

We scout tides and beach traffic in advance so your frames stay open and uncluttered. On the south shore we favor the calmer, sheltered coves at Conchas Chinas, where the surf is gentler for children and grandparents alike.

What To Expect

Wardrobe, the session day, and what arrives

A Vallarta family session is built to feel like the best evening of your trip rather than an appointment. We meet you on a bay beach or inside your resort about ninety minutes before sunset, start while there is still room to play in the sand, and move quietly through two or three nearby setups as the light deepens. We direct in English or Spanish, fold in the children's energy instead of fighting it, and keep grandparents seated and close to the action so no one is standing for long. Most families forget the camera is there within the first few minutes.

After booking you receive a wardrobe guide written for the warm, lush palette of the Pacific coast. The studio suggests coordinating, not matching, around two or three soft tones: ivory, sand, sage, terracotta and warm coral all sit beautifully against golden sand, jungle green and the colorful walls of the old town rather than competing with them. Flowing linens and cottons move well in the bay breeze, and barefoot reads better than shoes on the sand. We steer families away from neon, large logos and busy prints, and we are glad to review your outfits before the session so every generation looks like part of the same frame.

Because Banderas Bay faces west, we plan the entire session around the sunset, never the flat, hard light of midday. You leave with a private online gallery of fully edited, color-graded images in our warm editorial tone, delivered in one to three days, with pricing shared by inquiry so it fits your group and your time on the coast. Travel from Cancún is built into the quote. If your trip also touches the Caribbean, the same studio photographs the other coastline through our luxury family photography service.
