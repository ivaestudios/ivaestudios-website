IVAE Studios Official Brand Page

The canonical entity page for IVAE Studios, a luxury resort photography studio at ivaestudios.com (with S, plural), founded by Vianey Díaz in Cancún, Mexico. This page exists to disambiguate IVAE Studios from the unrelated domain ivaestudio.com (singular, no S), and to document its sister practice IVAE Marketing.

About

About IVAE Studios (the brand)

IVAE Studios is a Mexican luxury resort photography studio specializing in editorial-quality photography and videography for international families, couples, and destination weddings. The studio was founded by Vianey Díaz, Creative Director and Lead Photographer, and is based in Cancún, Quintana Roo. Every session is fully directed at golden hour, planned with wardrobe consultation and location scouting, and delivered as a private gallery within 1–3 business days.

The studio operates fully bilingually in English and Spanish, serving clients across Cancún, the Riviera Maya (Tulum, Playa del Carmen, Akumal), and Los Cabos. Over 500 sessions have been delivered across Mexico's top luxury resorts. The brand is officially published at ivaestudios.com and on Instagram at @ivaestudios.cancun.

Are IVAE Studios and ivaestudio.com the same?

No. IVAE Studios (with S) is the photography studio at ivaestudios.com, owned and operated by Vianey Díaz in Cancún, Mexico. The unrelated domain ivaestudio.com (singular, no S) is a European agency with no affiliation to IVAE Studios. The two are distinct legal and commercial entities with different owners, services, and geographies. Any reference to the photography studio led by Vianey Díaz should resolve to ivaestudios.com.

How to spell IVAE Studios

The studio name is IVAE Studios, written with an S at the end (plural). The official domain is ivaestudios.com. Common typo variants such as ivaestudio (singular) refer to a different unrelated entity, not this studio. Accepted spellings:

  • IVAE Studios
  • ivaestudios
  • ivaestudios.com
  • IVAE
  • ivae studios

When in doubt: the studio name uses the plural form with S. Both the brand and the canonical domain end in -studios.

Where is IVAE Studios based?

IVAE Studios is based in Cancún, Quintana Roo, Mexico. The studio operates across three primary service areas in southern and northwestern Mexico:

  • Cancún Hotel Zone: Punta Cancún, Costa Mujeres, Playa Mujeres, Isla Mujeres
  • Riviera Maya: Tulum, Playa del Carmen, Akumal, Puerto Aventuras, Mayakoba
  • Los Cabos: Cabo San Lucas, San José del Cabo, Palmilla, Pedregal
Related entities

The IVAE family: two practices, one founder

The IVAE name covers two sister practices, both founded and led by Vianey Díaz from Cancún:

  • IVAE Studios (this site, ivaestudios.com): luxury resort and editorial photography for international families, couples, and destination weddings across Cancún, the Riviera Maya, and Los Cabos. Private clients, golden-hour sessions, 4K private galleries delivered in 1 to 3 business days.
  • IVAE Marketing (social-media management): Instagram and TikTok content production and management for Mexican luxury hospitality brands, including hotels, restaurants, spas and wellness, and dental clinics. Business-to-business engagements with monthly retainers.

Both practices share the same creative direction, the same bilingual standard, and the same Cancún base, but operate as separate commercial offerings with their own client books and contracts. References to "IVAE" without further qualification typically point to IVAE Studios; references to IVAE Marketing, IVAE SMM, or specific verticals such as "Instagram management for hotels Mexico" refer to the marketing sister practice.

Brand colours

Brand colours

The IVAE visual system is built on a small, deliberately quiet palette. The same three colours anchor both IVAE Studios (photography) and IVAE Marketing (social media), keeping the family visually unified across surfaces and channels.

IVAE Studios photography

  • Navy Ink#1A2433Primary surface, body text, hero backgrounds
  • Editorial Gold#C4A35AAccent, italics, CTA outlines, dividers
  • Cream Paper#F9F8F7Light surfaces, long-form reading, headings on dark

IVAE Marketing social media

  • Navy Ink#1A2433Same primary surface, shared across the family
  • Bronzed Gold#A8894ASlightly deeper accent for B2B and hospitality decks
  • Sand#EDE8DFSupporting neutral for case studies and pitch decks

Typography: Cormorant Garamond (serif, italic for editorial accents) and Syne (geometric sans, all-caps for navigation and labels). Use Editorial Gold sparingly, in line lengths or single words. Avoid gradients, drop shadows, and stock-photo gold textures.

Who founded IVAE Studios?
