FAQ

Questions about Instagram in Cancun.

What does Instagram management in Cancun include?
Each plan includes on-site photo and video production, editorial calendar, captions in English and Spanish, scheduled posts, Reels, stories, daily community management and monthly reporting.
Do you produce Reels in-house?
Yes. Vertical video is shot, edited, captioned and scored in-house. Reels are not stitched from stock; they are produced on location.
How do you grow an Instagram account?
Growth comes from three levers: editorial consistency, distribution-friendly formats like Reels and carousels and active community management. We measure save rate, share rate and follower lift each month.
Can you manage an existing Instagram account, not a new one?
Yes. Most clients hand off an established account. We run a 30-day reset to align the feed, refresh the highlights and recalibrate the tone, then publish on schedule.
Do you handle paid Instagram ads?
Yes. Paid Instagram and Meta campaigns are included in the Enterprise plan, with creative production, targeting and reporting tied to revenue goals.
What businesses do you work with in Cancun?
Hotels, restaurants, spas, dental and aesthetic clinics, real estate developments and lifestyle brands. The aesthetic is calibrated per brand.
