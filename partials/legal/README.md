## What this is

A first-draft Privacy Policy and Terms of Service written in IVAE Studios'
editorial voice. Tone, structure, and bilingual coverage are production-ready;
the legal substance is **not**. Every page carries a visible `legal-disclaimer`
banner that names this directly.

## What still needs a real lawyer

| Section | Why it needs review |
|---|---|
| Privacy §4 — Image rights + galleries | Specific carve-out for portfolio use vs. private contracts. Photographer–client copyright defaults vary by jurisdiction. |
| Privacy §5 — Data retention | 24-month / 12-month numbers are studio-reasonable defaults; align with actual workflow + LFPDPPP. |
| Privacy §6 — Your rights | LFPDPPP (Mexico) and GDPR rights are **named** but the response procedure (DSAR workflow, response window) is generic. |
| Privacy §7 — Cross-border transfers | Mention of standard safeguards is editorial, not contractual. Identify actual processors (mail provider, hosting, gallery infra). |
| Privacy §8 — Security & breach | The 72-hour notice mirrors GDPR Art. 33 — confirm the studio can actually meet that. |
| Terms §3 — Booking + cancellation | Deposit %, rebooking window, force-majeure scope must mirror the real session contract the studio signs. |
| Terms §6 — Limitation of liability | "No liability beyond fees paid" is standard but enforceability depends on Mexican consumer-protection law (PROFECO). |
| Terms §8 — Governing law | Defaults to Mexican law / venue in México. Confirm with counsel that no carve-out is needed for international clients. |
