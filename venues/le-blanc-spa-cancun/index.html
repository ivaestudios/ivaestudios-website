About the Property

Adults-only luxury on the calm bay side

Le Blanc Spa Resort is a 5-star, all-inclusive, adults-only resort under the Palace Resorts brand, set on the calm bay side of Cancún Hotel Zone at km 10 of Boulevard Kukulcán. The architecture is white and minimalist throughout — marble, glass, and soft linen palettes that read beautifully on camera and pair naturally with editorial styling.

It is the right venue for elopements, intimate vow renewals, couples sessions and honeymoon portraits. Note: Le Blanc is not designed for large-group weddings — ceremony spaces are limited by design and the property leans toward private, romantic experiences rather than 150-guest receptions. For larger destination weddings we recommend the JW Marriott or one of the open-Caribbean-side properties.

Light & Timing

How light moves at Le Blanc

Sunrise — the signature window

The beach faces east toward the open Caribbean and the bay water is calm year-round. We schedule the strongest portraits between 6:30 and 7:30 AM, with the first 20 minutes after sunrise giving the cleanest editorial light. Empty beach, soft pink skies, no crowds.

Sunset over the lagoon

Because Le Blanc sits on the bay side, sunset is a backlit silhouette window across Laguna Nichupté rather than over the Caribbean. We use it for cinematic side-lit frames on the lagoon-facing terraces and for blue-hour pool deck images.

Midday — avoided

We never schedule between 11 AM and 3 PM — the overhead Cancún sun produces harsh shadows, blown-out skies, and heat glare that no retouch saves. If your day requires a midday window, we move indoors to the marble lobby or shaded spa garden.

Season-by-season

November-April brings dry-season clarity — the most reliable sunrise palettes. May-October trades a few weather-contingent days for warmer color and quieter resort traffic. We monitor conditions for 72 hours before each session and shift the call time when needed.
