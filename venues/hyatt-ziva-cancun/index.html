About the Property

Family-focused all-inclusive on the peninsula tip

Hyatt Ziva Cancún is a 547-room all-inclusive family resort at km 19.5 on the southernmost peninsula of Cancún Hotel Zone. The peninsula tip is the property's defining feature — waves on three sides, dramatic open horizons, and a sense of being on a private headland that no other Hotel Zone resort can offer.

Best for family vacation portraits, multi-generational gatherings, and small destination weddings of 50-100 guests. The feel is different from the adults-only resorts: energetic, occasionally family-loud, and unmistakably joyful. We schedule sessions early in the morning when the property is at its quietest and the geography is at its most cinematic.

Where We Shoot

Light & Timing

How light moves at Hyatt Ziva

Three-direction peninsula

The peninsula tip lets us shoot east toward the open Caribbean, south down the coast, or west over the Hotel Zone. We choose the angle by season — winter favors south-east, summer favors a north-east frame to keep the sun behind the subject.

Sunrise — quiet and clean

The east-facing main beach is at its quietest weekday mornings before 8 AM. Sunrise (06:30-07:30) is the strongest editorial window before family pool noise picks up. Strongly recommended for portraits.

Sunset

Sunset over the lagoon and southern Hotel Zone gives a backlit silhouette window from the western terraces. We use it for wedding ceremonies and golden-hour family frames.

Crowds & rhythm

Hyatt Ziva runs energetic and family-loud through midday — schedule formal portraits at sunrise or after sunset. We coordinate with the wedding planner and concierge to lock the gazebo and peninsula tip during your booked window.
