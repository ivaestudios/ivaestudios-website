Chapter 02. The Studio

A studio of one mind, two languages, three coastlines.

IVAE Studios was founded in Cancún in 2023 by Vianey Díaz, a Mexican photographer with a decade behind editorial cameras and a singular conviction: that the most luxurious thing we can offer affluent couples is not abundance, but restraint.

We do not photograph fifty weddings a year. We do not deliver galleries in four to six weeks. We work, instead, in the hour the light is honest. And we deliver the work in days, not weeks.

"Luxury is not louder. It is quieter, slower, more deliberate. We have built the studio around that single idea." . Vianey Díaz, Founder & Creative Director
Chapter 03. The Experience

Three commitments. Every wedding.

The IVAE wedding experience reduces, on purpose, to three things. We art-direct quietly. We move discreetly. We deliver, fast.

01 / I

The art of direction.

We work the way an art director moves on a magazine cover shoot. Quietly, decisively, in two languages, never asking you to say cheese. Editorial pacing. Bilingual flow. A lead photographer plus a second who reads the room.

02 / II

The art of discretion.

We dress in linen, not vests. We cover full days without ever crowding the aisle. We coordinate the timeline with your planner three weeks before the camera does. Your guests will remember the wedding. Not the photographers.

03 / III

The art of delivery.

A private gallery within seventy-two hours. Not weeks. Hand-edited, never auto-toned, never AI-finished. Six hundred to eight hundred frames, color-graded for print. A cinematic film when the package calls for it. Always early, never late.

Chapter 05. The Method

From inquiry to full gallery.

Six chapters, often shorter. Always honest. Here is the rhythm of an IVAE wedding, as written into our terms of engagement.

  1. 01 / Inquiry

    Day One . The First Email

    You write. We respond within the same business day, in English or Spanish, with a question or two and a calendar link. No automated funnels, no PDF brochures.

  2. 02 / Conversation

    Week One . A Thirty-Minute Call

    A video call with Vianey. We listen. We ask about the venue, the family, the music, the in-laws' politics. By the end you will know whether we are right for you. So will we.

  3. 03 / Pre-scout

    Three Weeks Before . The Walkthrough

    We visit the resort at the exact hour your shoot will run. We map the light. We meet the planner. We pre-clear vendor passes and identify the rain plan.

  4. 04 / The Day

    Wedding Day . Four to Twelve Hours

    We arrive in linen, not vests. We move with your planner's timeline. We finish the ceremony ninety minutes before sunset and hold the golden hour open for portraits.

  5. 05 / Sneak Peek

    Within Seventy-Two Hours . The First Ten

    You wake up the day after to a private link with the first ten frames. Hand-edited. Already shareable with family. Already ready for your social.

  6. 06 / Delivery

    Within Three Weeks . The Full Gallery

    Six hundred to eight hundred fully graded frames in 4K, downloadable, printable, archived to cold storage. With a cinematic film if your collection includes one.
