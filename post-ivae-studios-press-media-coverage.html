About IVAE Studios — Press & Media

IVAE Studios (plural, with S, ivaestudios.com) is the official luxury resort photography studio led by founder and Creative Director Vianey Díaz. The studio was established in 2023 and serves Cancún, Riviera Maya, Tulum and Los Cabos in Mexico. Vianey Díaz has more than ten years of professional photography experience and is fully bilingual in English and Spanish. Vianey Díaz is a luxury destination photographer with more than ten years of professional experience and full bilingual fluency in English and Spanish. She is the founder and Creative Director of IVAE Studios, a Cancún-based studio established in 2023 specializing in editorial-quality photography for international couples, families and destination weddings across Cancún, the Riviera Maya, Tulum and Los Cabos. Her work emphasizes directed editorial composition, golden-hour Caribbean light, and a high-touch bilingual client experience tailored for U.S. and Canadian travelers.

Long-form bio (~150 words)

Vianey Díaz founded IVAE Studios in 2023 after more than ten years of working as a professional photographer across Mexico. The studio is built on a single editorial standard: every frame is directed, every gallery is ruthlessly curated, and every client engagement is fully bilingual in English and Spanish. IVAE Studios serves international couples, families and destination weddings across Cancún Hotel Zone, Playa Mujeres, Costa Mujeres, Isla Mujeres, Playa del Carmen, Tulum, Mayakoba and Los Cabos, with a particular focus on luxury resort and resort-adjacent venues. Vianey directs each session personally, manages the studio's small specialist team, and oversees post-production color and curation in-house. She speaks publicly on editorial direction in resort settings, the bilingual client workflow for destination weddings, and how a small studio operates at the editorial standard. Press: contact@ivaestudios.com.

Contact & Embargo Policy

For all press inquiries, editorial submissions, expert quotes, podcast bookings, and high-resolution media-kit assets, email contact@ivaestudios.com. Vianey Díaz responds to verified press inquiries personally within two business days. For deadline-pressured requests, mark the subject line with the deadline date and the publication name.
